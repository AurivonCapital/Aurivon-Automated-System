THE MEMOIR.

I

remember the first time the market truly humbled me. It wasn't just a loss; it was a lesson in the brutal reality of capital. That was the day Randall Goh stopped being a dreamer and started being a practitioner.

Aurivon Capital wasn't built in a boardroom. It was forged in the 4:00 AM silence of London sessions and the high-octane chaos of New York opens. Every rule we have now—every drawdown limit, every lot size restriction—was written in the blood of previous trades.

This firm is my legacy. It is a place for those who understand that in the world of high-finance, your word is your bond, but your data is your truth.

Randall Goh
Founder, Aurivon Capital
